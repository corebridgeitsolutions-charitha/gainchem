About Us

GAIN Chem Industries is a leading manufacturing company in the home care cleaning products sector in Sri Lanka. Our manufacturing facility is located at No. 135, Maloes Lake, Kuda Uduwa, Horana.

GAIN Chem Industries is a single-owner company founded by its CEO, Mr. Nuwan Kulathunga, who provides direct supervision over the entire manufacturing process and is responsible for all administrative functions and workforce management. The company operates a dedicated production plant for home care product manufacturing, where both management and employees have acquired extensive experience and expertise in production operations and quality control procedures.

GAIN Chem products are in high demand across the Sri Lankan market, including hospitals, leading automobile companies, and garment factories. In addition, we serve as a gateway for international manufacturers and product solution providers supplying raw materials, supporting them in initiating, establishing, and expanding their presence in the Sri Lankan market.

The rapid growth of GAIN Chem Industries has positioned the company as an unrivalled player within the current supply chain, driven by superior product quality, innovation, reliability, and a resilient, highly committed team. We are dedicated to delivering the best home care products to the Sri Lankan market while upholding strict corporate governance and social responsibility to better serve humanity.
